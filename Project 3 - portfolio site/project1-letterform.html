Channeling personality into typography
I immediately fell in love with this font. Tajawal, or “to wander” in arabic, is an online travel platform based in the UAE. Its bespoke font is rooted in the branding concept for the company: youthful and dynamic design and travel. To reflect that, the forms are broken into geometric shapes reflect the colorful fundamentals of design. To symbolize growth, the shapes point in different directions to emulate the boundless, potential of travel.

Boutros design house is London-based type foundry connecting global clients with the Arabic market through bespoke typefaces, lettering, and calligraphy. They serve media outlets and companies with worldwide audiences such as BBC Arabic TV channel, IBM, Apple, AlJazirah Newspaper, and many more.
